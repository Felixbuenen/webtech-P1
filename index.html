The story begins

Harry Potter is a 10 year old boy who lives at Privet Drive, along with his horrible aunt, uncle and cousin. Life is not very fascinating, as he spends a lot of time in a cupboard. One day, however, he receives a letter from Hogwarts: a school for witchcraft and wizardry. Harry's uncle forces him to stay home, until the half-giant Hagrid comes to take him personally. Harry appears to be famous in the wizarding world, due to his connection with the evil Lord Voldemort. He soon finds out Hogwarts is not just a place with friends...

Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone is the first book in a series of classics. Having won many awards, J.K. Rowling takes the reader on a journey through the rich and exciting world of Harry Potter, which is a treat for both children and adults alike.
